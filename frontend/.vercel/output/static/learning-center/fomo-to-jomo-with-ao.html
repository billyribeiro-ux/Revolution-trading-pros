Psychology: Turning FOMO into JOMO

Allison Ostrander
38:00
November 14, 2022

Fear of Missing Out (FOMO) is one of the most dangerous emotions in trading. In this session, Allison Ostrander teaches you how to transform that anxiety into Joy of Missing Out (JOMO) - the peace that comes from disciplined trading.

What you'll learn:

• Understanding the psychology behind FOMO

• How FOMO leads to poor trading decisions

• Techniques for emotional regulation

• Building a JOMO mindset

• Creating rules that eliminate emotional trading

• Real-world examples and case studies
