How We Trade Under $25k

    SPX 0DTE Leverage

    The S&P 500 index offers massive liquidity. We scalp quick moves (10-30 minutes) using 0DTE options that offer high percentage returns on small capital.

    We never bet the farm. We teach you to risk strictly 1-2% of your account per trade, ensuring you survive the learning curve.
